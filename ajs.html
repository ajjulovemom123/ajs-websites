About Shanks

The Legendary Yonko

Shanks, also known as "Red-Haired" Shanks, is the chief of the Red Hair Pirates and one of the Four Emperors who rule over the New World. He is a powerful and influential figure whose actions have shaped the course of the Great Pirate Era.

Despite being a pirate, Shanks follows a unique moral code. He values peace and will go to great lengths to prevent unnecessary conflict, as demonstrated when he stopped the Marineford War with his mere presence.

His connection to Gol D. Roger, the Pirate King, and his mentorship of Monkey D. Luffy make him a pivotal character in the world's balance of power.

Powers & Abilities

Haki Mastery

Master of all three types of Haki, including the rare and powerful Conqueror's Haki that can knock out opponents with his willpower alone.

Swordsmanship

Extremely skilled swordsman capable of clashing evenly with the world's strongest swordsmen using his named sword, Gryphon.

Diplomatic Influence

Respected by both pirates and the World Government, able to end wars and negotiate with the highest authorities through his reputation alone.

Crew Leadership

Commands one of the most balanced and powerful pirate crews in the world, with exceptionally skilled fighters and a loyal following.
